You travel upstream

After walking upstream a short way you seem to find yourself already arriving at a large river-side port. Soon you realize the noise you hear is not coming from a busy port, but rather a hospital room where you can vaugly hear a doctor talking to what you think might be your wife. This is when you realize your situation is not real, and you are actually deep in a months long coma.

Fight for your life, push through, WAKE UP..
